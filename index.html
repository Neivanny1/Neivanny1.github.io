Motivation

As a music enthusiast with an eclectic taste, I felt frustrated by the limitations of existing streaming platforms.

Their recommendations were predictable, often suggesting mainstream hits or artists similar to ones they already knew. I craved musical adventures, a chance to discover hidden gems from across genres and time periods. Fueled by this desire, I ventured into the world of data science and music analysis. After months of experimentation, I created a recommendation system that went beyond simple popularity metrics. The result? A personalized playlist experience that felt like having a musical soulmate curate their listening journey.

About Us

Mood magic is User Friendly Website design to bring and cheer up people moods by recommnding songs to them.

For team work and Quick talent sport we developed a machine learning model that recommneds songs to user based on the dataset it has been trained on. The algrothim recommneds songs to user which upon clicking it searches for song from youtube and tries to play it.
